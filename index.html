About me

Greetings online visitor, you can call me Troy! I am a Computer Science Graduate Student and a Web Developer that shifted careers from Architecture to Web Development.

The way I see it, the main factor that molds our life today is technology. As we shifted to a more technological society at the start of this pandemic, my love for the Architecture profession faded but my love for coding and developing sites, applications, etc. grew tremendously. My strong desire to innovate people's lives through code became the backbone for my hardwork and patience in learning and enhancing my skills in code. For now my main purpose is to grow, and learn from actual industry experience. Establishing myself as a Web Developer in the industry is just the first step as my hunger for knowledge in tech branches out to blockchain and Machine Learning as well.

Read my blog on How the Pandemic Redirected my Life from Architecture to Technology as I expound more on how my story started from Architecture to Technology.
